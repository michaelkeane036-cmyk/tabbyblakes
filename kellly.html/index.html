About the Festival

Baltimore Afro Food Festival 2026 is a one-day outdoor celebration of African and Afro-diaspora cuisine, music, and culture scheduled for Saturday, July 25, 2026, in Baltimore, Maryland.

The festival will highlight Black-owned restaurants, caterers, chefs, food trucks, and cultural vendors while offering families and young professionals a safe, vibrant Independence Day experience that centers African and Caribbean flavors.
